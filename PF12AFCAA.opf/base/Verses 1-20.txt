||

vipulaḥ3 paramo4 ’tyanto5 ’viparyastaś6 ca cetasi |upakārāśayaḥ sthānaṃ yāne ’smin guṇapūritaḥ
||

dānaṃ pāramitāṣatkam āmiṣābhayadharmataḥ |ekadvayatrayeṇeha pratipat sā ’pratiṣṭhitā ||7

ātmabhāve8 pratikṛtau9 vipāke10 cāpy asaktatā |apravṛttitadanyārthapravṛttiparivarjane ||

pragraho maṇḍale tredhā nimittāc cittavāraṇam |uttarottarasaṃdehajanmataś ca nivāraṇā ||11

saṃskṛtatvena saṃkalpya saṃpat prāptau nivāryate |trailakṣaṇyānyathābhāvāt tadabhāvāt tathāgataḥ
||

sahetuphalagāmbhīryadeśanāsmin yugādhame |na niṣphalā yataḥ santi bodhisattvās trayānvitāḥ ||

śikṣayopāsānāt pūrvaṃ kuśalasyāvaropaṇāt |śīlavanto ’nyabuddheṣu guṇavantaś ca kīrtitāḥ ||

sapudgaleṣu dharmeṣu saṃjñāyā viprahāṇataḥ |prajñāvantaś ca saṃjñāyā aṣṭadhāṣṭārthabhedataḥ
||

pṛthagbhāvena saṃtatyā vṛtter ājīvitasthiteḥ |punaś ca gatilīnatvād ātmasaṃjñā caturvidhā ||

sarvābhāvād abhāvasya sadbhāvān nābhilāpyataḥ |abhilāpaprayogāc ca dharmasaṃjñā caturvidhā ||

adhimuktivaśāt teṣāṃ bhūtasaṃjñā prasādataḥ |yathārutāgrahāt saṃyagdeśitatvasya codgrahāt ||

phalato na mitā buddhaiḥ praṇidhijñānalakṣitāḥ |lābhasatkārakāmānāṃ tadvādavinivṛttaye ||

asthānād ānukūlyāc ca dharmeṣv adhigamasya hi |kolasyeva parityāgo dharme saṃdhis tato mataḥ
||

nairmāṇikena no buddho dharmo nāpi ca deśitaḥ |deśitas tu dvayāgrāhyo ’vācyo ’vākpathalakṣaṇāt
||

grahaṇadeśaṇā cāsya nāpārthā puṇyasaṃgrahāt |puṇyaṃ bodhyanupastaṃbhād upastaṃbhād dvayasya
ca ||

svābhāvikāptihetutvāt tadanyasya ca janmanaḥ |kaivalyād buddhadharmāṇām agryatvaṃ puṇyasādhanam
||

agrāhyānabhilapyatvaṃ svaphalānām anudgrahāt |dvayāvaraṇanirmokṣāt subhūtāv araṇādvayaṃ ||

buddhadīpaṃkarāgrāhād vakyenādhigamasya hi |tataś cādhigame siddhā agrāhyānabhilāpyatā ||
